What Is MealBets

Our Vision

Solve the lunch dilemma, and make lunch a joyful surprise for every workday.

Our Mission

MealBets is the first of its kind in the market to provide a surprise box lunch subscription plan.

We deliver high quality, healthy lunch to customer's hands on time, with luxury surprise to give everyone a joyful lunch experience.

We aim to relieve people from the traditional time-consuming, tedious, and anxious ways to lunch.

By leveraging our innovative virtual restaurants, low-cost batch delivery model, and a strong evolving software system, we are built to achieve these goals.
