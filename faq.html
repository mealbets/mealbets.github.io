Terms & Ordering Questions

This page includes some frequently asked questions and answers. If you still have questions or concerns, please feel free to reach us.

MealBets provides a mobile app for ordering school lunches. Meals are delivered to schools with no delivery fee.

Download the MealBets app, create an account, and order directly in the app.

Go to My Orders > Upcoming in the app. The latest cancellation time is 2:00 PM on the day before delivery. After the cutoff, orders can’t be changed.

Meals are free from nuts, tree nuts, and shellfish, and all meals (except Deluxe Full Meal) are fully PPM-150 compliant.
Our food quality and safety standards exceed industry norms.

At this time, we’re unable to accommodate custom ingredient requests, as meals are prepared in batches.
However, each meal’s Details section clearly lists the main ingredients and dietary restrictions.
Please choose the options that best meet your child’s needs.
If you have any questions, our support team is happy to help confirm ingredient information.

All meals are prepared following strict standard operating procedures (SOPs).

  • Asian Cuisine & World’s Flavours meals are portioned by weight (Protein, vegetables, and carbohydrates are scaled proportionally for each size.):
  • XS: 260g
  • S: 350g
  • L: 450g
  • XL: 550g
  • Prime Sushi meals are portioned by piece count:
  • Handrolls: quantity is shown by the number of rolls (1 roll = 4 sushi pieces)
  • Sushi roll sets: the total number of sushi pieces is clearly specified

This ensures consistent portions and balanced meals across all sizes.

Pricing varies by meal and size, starting from $7.49. All prices are shown in the app.

We currently offer five categories: Asian Cuisine, World's Flavors, Prime Sushi, Burger/Sandwich, and Full Meals. More options will be added over time.
