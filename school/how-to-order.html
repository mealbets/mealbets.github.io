How to Order

  • Downlgad the "MealBets" app from the App Store and register an account.

  • Once inside, go to "My Account," click on "Meal Notes," and add a note for each student meal. Use the format "Grade - Teacher's Name - Child's Full name" to avoid confusion (e.g., G3-MsBrown-Jack Lin).

  • After setup, you will see the tables under the "School" tag on the homepage, including Asian and Western cuisines. Simply choose and book as needed. Sizes and prices are as below (Price might be higher for schools with fundraising needs):

    • Small : From $7.99 + HST, recommended for JK~G3, approximately 350g
    • Large : From $9.99 + HST, recommended for G4~G8, approximately 450g
    • XL: From $11.99 + HST, recommended for G9~G12, approximately 550g
  • Click on the corresponding table: Select date - Select meal note - Select delivery address. Make sure to choose Your Child's School as the address.
  • Add to cart - Complete payment. Finally, double-check your order details under "My Orders" to ensure everything is correct.

Important

Orders or cancellations must be submitted or canceled by 2 PM the day before the latest delivery date. Orders cannot be manually processed or canceled after this time.
