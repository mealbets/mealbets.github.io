How to Order

  • Download the "MealBets" app from the App Store and register an account.

  • Once inside, go to "My Account", click "Meal Notes", add a note and its corresponding school for each student. Use the format "Grade - Teacher's Name - Child's Full Name" to avoid ambiguity (e.g. G3-MsBrown-Jack Lin). Click "Confirm" to save the meal notes.

  • After setup, go back to "Home" in the App, select school (or camp) tab.
    Select Your Child's Note and School, you will see all the available dates under the tab,
    Choose a date and under it all available food options will show, you can click details to see pictures, main ingredients, and allergens, etc.
    Choose the size and option, the price will be correspondingly updated. Some of the sample prices are as below (Price might be higher for schools with fundraising programs):

    • XS : From $7.49, recommended for JK~SK, approximately 260g
    • Small : From $8.99, recommended for G1~G3, approximately 350g
    • Large : From $10.99, recommended for G4~G8, approximately 450g
    • XL: From $12.99, recommended for G9~G12, approximately 550g
  • Add to cart - Complete payment. Finally, double-check your order details under "My Orders" to ensure everything is correct. If you find anything wrong, please let us know ASAP, so we can correct it for you.

Important

Orders or cancellations must be submitted or canceled by 2 PM the day before the latest delivery date. Orders cannot be manually processed or canceled after this time.
